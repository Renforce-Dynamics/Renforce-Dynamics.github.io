Building humanoid intelligence from the ground up.

We advance humanoid motion intelligence: agile bipedal locomotion, reactive balance, and contact-rich control in unstructured environments. We believe robot intelligence grows bottom-up, from robust physical controllers to emergent behavior through interaction and experience.

Focus: dynamic locomotion, parkour-like agility, whole-body balance, and sim-to-real transfer of contact-rich skills.

Our Approach

Intelligence from the bottom up: master physical interaction first, then scale behavior through learning and experience.

Dynamic Whole-Body Locomotion

Controllers for walking, running, jumping, vaulting, and balance recovery over uneven terrain and obstacles.

Bottom-Up Skill Acquisition

RL, motion imitation, and skill composition to grow behaviors from simple primitives to complex, chained maneuvers.

Sim-to-Real Transfer

Domain randomization, actuator modeling, and closed-loop perception to deploy policies on real humanoid hardware.

We are an open robotics team exploring the full stack of embodied intelligence: scalable reinforcement learning, foundation behavior priors, predictive world models, and closed-loop sim-to-real deployment.

Current focus: multi-robot coordination, lifelong skill acquisition, and robust autonomy on resource-constrained hardware.
